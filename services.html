What We Offer

Our Services

A complete spectrum of interior design and fit-out services — from the first concept to the final furnishing.

Full Service Studio

Every Space, Every Scale

Whether you're furnishing a single room or fitting out an entire office campus, Optimum Interiors has the expertise, team and resources to deliver. We handle everything in-house — so you deal with one team from start to finish.

01
Design & Fit-Out

Commercial Interior Design

We design and deliver complete commercial interiors — from boutique retail stores to multi-floor office fit-outs. Our commercial team handles every aspect of the project: concept development, space planning, material procurement, construction coordination and final fit-out, ensuring a seamless process and stunning result.

  • Office fit-outs & refurbishments
  • Retail design & visual merchandising
  • Restaurant & café interiors
  • Brand-aligned design concepts
  • Project management end-to-end
02
Your Home, Reimagined

Residential Interior Design

Your home should be a reflection of who you are. Our residential design team works closely with you to understand your lifestyle, tastes and needs — then creates living spaces that are both beautifully designed and deeply personal. From a single bedroom refresh to a complete home transformation.

  • Living room & dining design
  • Kitchen design & cabinetry
  • Master bedroom & en-suite design
  • Children's rooms & nurseries
  • Outdoor living spaces
03
Guest-First Design

Hospitality Interior Design

In hospitality, first impressions are everything. We create hotels, resorts, restaurants and bar interiors that capture the imagination and keep guests coming back. Our hospitality designs balance brand identity, operational efficiency and the kind of atmosphere that earns five-star reviews.

  • Hotel lobbies & guest rooms
  • Restaurant & bar fit-outs
  • Resort common area design
  • Event space & banquet design
  • Spa & wellness interiors
04
Healing Environments

Healthcare Interior Design

Healthcare environments must do more than look good — they must function flawlessly and create a sense of calm for patients, families and staff. Our healthcare design team brings specialised knowledge of infection control, patient flow, wayfinding and the psychology of healing spaces.

  • Clinic & hospital design
  • Dental & specialist practice fit-outs
  • Wellness centre interiors
  • Pharmacy & reception design
  • Compliant materials & finishes
05
Brand-Reflective Spaces

Corporate Interior Design

Your office communicates your brand values before anyone says a word. We design corporate environments — from executive suites to open-plan workstations — that inspire productivity, reflect your identity and impress every client who walks through the door.

  • Executive offices & boardrooms
  • Reception & lobby design
  • Open-plan workstation layouts
  • Breakout & collaboration spaces
  • Brand-embedded design systems
06
Flexible Workspace Solutions

Office Partitioning

Transform open floors into productive, private and flexible workspaces with our premium partition systems. We supply and install a full range of aluminium and glass partition solutions — from single-glazed systems to fully frameless glass walls — at highly competitive prices with expert installation.

  • Aluminium frame glass partitions
  • Frameless glass partition walls
  • Frosted & manifestation glass
  • Demountable partition systems
  • Custom acoustic partitions
07
Optimise Every Metre

Space Planning

Good space planning is the foundation of every successful interior. Our spatial planners conduct thorough assessments of your existing space and produce optimised layouts that improve flow, efficiency and wellbeing. We plan for how people actually use spaces — not just how they look on paper.

  • Residential room planning
  • Home office & kitchen planning
  • Bathroom & storage planning
  • Commercial floor plate optimisation
  • Full spatial assessment reports
08
Colour & Texture

Painting & Special Effects

A fresh coat of paint can transform any space — but our painters go far beyond standard finishes. We offer decorative painting, textured coatings, faux finishes, murals and specialist wall treatments that add depth, character and luxury to both residential and commercial interiors.

  • Interior painting (residential & commercial)
  • Decorative & feature wall treatments
  • Venetian plaster & stucco finishes
  • Geometric & pattern painting
  • Colour consultation & mood boards
