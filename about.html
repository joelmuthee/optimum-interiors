About Optimum Interiors

Crafting Spaces That
Tell Your Story

Founded in Nairobi, Optimum Interiors Limited has been at the forefront of interior design excellence for over five years. We are a team of creative designers, spatial planners and project managers united by one purpose: delivering spaces that inspire, function beautifully and stand the test of time.

From our studios in Westlands and Karen, we serve clients across Nairobi and beyond — from intimate residential spaces to large-scale commercial and hospitality fit-outs. Every project, regardless of scale, receives the same level of care, craftsmanship and commitment.

We believe great design is about more than aesthetics. It's about understanding how people live, work and move through space — and then engineering an environment that enhances every moment of that experience.

"Our mission is to create spaces that enhance the lives of our clients by combining functionality, beauty, and comfort."
