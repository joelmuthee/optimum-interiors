Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Optimum Interiors? +

Simply fill in the contact form, call us, or send a WhatsApp message. We'll schedule a free initial consultation — either at your space or at one of our studios — to discuss your vision, timeline and budget. There's no obligation.

What areas do you serve? +

Our primary market is Greater Nairobi, including Westlands, Karen, Kilimani, Lavington, Runda, Muthaiga, CBD and surrounding areas. We also take on projects in other Kenyan cities for the right brief — please enquire.

How long does a typical project take? +

It depends on scope. A single room refresh can be completed in 2–4 weeks. A full apartment redesign typically takes 6–10 weeks. Large commercial fit-outs range from 8 to 20+ weeks. We provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project.

Do you handle procurement and installation? +

Yes. We are a full-service studio. We handle design, material sourcing, procurement, supplier coordination, project management and final installation. You deal with one team throughout the entire process.

Can you work within a tight budget? +

Absolutely. We design for all budgets and are transparent about costs from the very beginning. We'll always find creative ways to maximise impact within your investment — and we never recommend more than you need.
